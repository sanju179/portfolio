    Projects

    A comprehensible and easily navigable software was developed to help ease small scale clinics out of their traditional register system into today's world of convenience. With 3 user modes, admins can manage all the accounts of the clinic's staff, while the receptionists can admit a patient (old or new) for a consultation quickly, depending on their state of emergency. Doctors can enter medical data, view the patient queue and accordingly save time and resources. This software was developed in a scrum team.

    Developed a web application that incorporated front-end design, rerouting algorithms, Flask framework and real-time traffic data APIs to suggest optimal routes, based on an assumed dataset. Users can look up for the most time-efficient route from a source to a destination, which is entirely calculated based on real-time traffic in the area with factors such as distance and flow of traffic.

    Developed an application that generates melodies that dynamically adapt to the user's liking. Primarily utilises Genetic Algorithms to alter musical sequences, trains and predicts how well a user may like the generated melody using a Random Forest Classifier and incorporates Gradient Descent algorithms to calculate fitness of musical sequences.
